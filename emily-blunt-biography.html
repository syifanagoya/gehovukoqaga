Who is Emily Blunt?

Emily Blunt is a British-American actress who has won the hearts of millions because of her exceptional acting skills. Born on February 23, 1983, she is known as an actress who portrays women’s characters beautifully from all wake of life. She was interested in acting from a very early age.

Emily’s full name is Emily Olivia Leah Blunt, and she was born and raised in London, England. She is the Golden Globe Award winner for Best Supporting Actress and various other awards and nominations.

Blunt is best known for her supporting role in The Devil Wears Prada. In the film, she was comparatively a new face where famous actresses like Meryl Streep and Anna Hathaway were the leading character. However, Emily’s unparalleled performance didn’t go unnoticed. This award-winning performance of hers took her acting career to a greater height.

Emily Blunt has also delivered her fantastic work as an actress in movies such as A Quiet Place, My Summer of Love, Salmon Fishing in the Yemen, Into the Woods, and others. However, the most appreciable aspect of her work is that she is not restricted to any single type of character. Instead, she experiments with a variety of characters, and what’s more, she delivers every single character perfectly.

She is a woman who seems calm, poised, and confident. One would think she was always this self-confident lady. However, would you believe if we said Emily, as a child, had a problem with stuttering? Furthermore, it was through acting that she finally overcame this problem. To know more about Emily’s unknown facts, scroll down further.

Interesting Facts

1. Emily Blunt had a stuttering problem as a kid

Emily had a problem with stuttering since she was 6 years old. She lived with the issue for another seven years. But, then, how does she speak so clearly, you may wonder. Here’s the reason why. Emily had a teacher who suggested participating in a school show in a different accent. And guess what, it worked! Emily is one of those few celebrities who openly talk about such problems. As she mentioned in one of the articles about her stuttering, “I was a smart kid and had a lot to say, but I couldn’t say it. It would just haunt me. I never thought I’d be able to sit and talk to someone like I’m talking to you right now.”

2. Blunt is exceptionally good at singing, playing cello and horse-riding

If you thought Emily was a brilliant actor, wait until you hear about her other talents. She is a wonderful singer, an amazing cello player, and a killer horse-rider! Isn’t she a complete package? Emily did her own singing in her movie Marry Poppins. Yeah, she is that good.

3. Emily had to be wooed by Dwayne Johnson for her role in Jungle Cruise

Emily Blunt was adamant about not acting in one of her recent films Jungle Cruise. However, she was sent a very “cute” video by her co-star Dwayne Johnson requesting her to take the role. So, she finally accepted the role though the amazing script was also a primary reason.

4. She has a nickname for Dwayne Johson

The Jungle Cruise co-stars have literally turned into best friends. Did you know Emily even has a nickname for Dwayne? She has him nick-named “Toots.” Emily also says that she likes giving Dwayne cute nicknames.

Emily Blunt Early Life and Childhood

Emily Blunt was born on February 23, 1983, in the London Borough of Wandsworth. She was born as the second child to Oliver Simon Peter and Joanna. She was born in a prominent family. Her father Oliver was a barrister, and her mother was an actress and teacher. Not only that, her grandfather was Major General Peter Blunt, and her uncle is a well–known Parliament Member, Crispin Blunt.

Emily has three siblings- Felicity Blunt, Sebastian Blunt, and Susannah Blunt.

She did her schooling in Ibstock Place School. Thereafter, she attended “Hurtwood House,” a private high school for residential students in Dorking. There she completed her two years course on performing arts.

Emily had a stuttering problem as a kid. She struggled with this problem for seven years. Her parents took her to see many therapists but to no avail. She credits one of her teachers for helping her finally overcome her problem. It was her idea that she part takes in a school art. As Emily later put on, she felt liberated when she became a different character that helped her overcome her problem.

Born on 8 March 1951, Oliver Blunt is a barrister in the United Kingdom. He did his schooling at Bedford School, Bedfordshire, England. Similarly, he graduated from the University of Southampton. He is one of the highest-paid barristers of the United Kingdom.

Joanna Margaret Blunt (Mother)

Joanna Margaret Blunt is a famous actress. She was known as Joanna Mackie. She was known for her role in movies like Lillie, Fighting Back, and BreakPoint. Joanna later gave up her acting career and took to teaching.

So, we can say that Emily’s mother had a significant role in her acting career. Various pictures have shown that the mother-daughter share a very close bond.

Career

Blunt took to acting as a way to overcome her stuttering problem. However, her natural acting talent took her a long way.

Emily started her acting career at a young age in a West End Production The Royal Family. Then, she made her film debut with the lead role in the movie My Summer of Love. Finally, her realistic role as a mentally troubled young girl in Gideon’s Daughter’s BBC television film won her the Golden Globe’s Award For Best Supporting Actress.

However, her supporting role in the movie The Devil Wears Prada was critically acclaimed. This role took her career to an even greater height.

After this role, she has appeared as the lead actress in many movies that have gained equal recognition and appreciation. Her movies like The Young Victoria, Salmon Fishing in the Yemen, Edge of Tomorrow, A Quiet Place have shown her diversity as an actress. The best thing about her roles in these movies is her realistic and perfect portrayal of these characters.

Awards

Emily Blunt has given many unforgettable movies, and her work in each of them is equally appreciated. This is the reason that she has received numerous awards and recognition for her work.

She has received prestigious awards such as the Golden Globe’s Award for the best-supporting actress, Critics’ Choice Movie Award, Satellite Awards, Saturn Awards, SAG Awards, Gotham Awards.

Similarly, she has also been nominated for numerous awards such as the BAFTA Awards, AACTA Awards, Empire Awards, etc.
